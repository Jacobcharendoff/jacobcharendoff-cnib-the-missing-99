Meet iris.

iris.

Your guide for life with sight loss.
So you can get back to living.

When sight loss changes everything, knowing what's available to you shouldn't be another thing to figure out alone.

iris. is CNIB's member engagement guide. iris. gets to know each member — their diagnosis, their location, their stage of life, what matters to them — and connects them to the programs, community, and support built for exactly where they are. Personally. Specifically. At no cost.

Powered by CNIB. Built for the 1.5 million.
The Model

Two journeys.
One loop.

iris. doesn't just help CNIB find new members. iris. helps CNIB build a community that regenerates itself — where the people we reach become the people who help others.

Journey 1
Canadians with sight loss
01
Acquire — Finding the door
However someone arrives, iris. is there.
A doctor says the words. A parent Googles at 2am. A social worker passes a card. No form. No waitlist. No phone tree. iris. opens every conversation the same way — "I'm so glad you found me. What's been going on?"
02
Engage — A conversation that goes somewhere
iris. listens first, then points to the next step.
Diagnosis, city, family, fear — iris. hears the whole story. Then surfaces the two or three CNIB programs that are actually right for this person, at this moment. Doesn't end with information — ends with action. A referral booked. A peer scheduled. A call back tomorrow.
03
Retain — The guide who stays
Sight loss doesn't end. Neither does iris.
As life changes, iris. checks in. A new need surfaces — iris. has a new program. A hard week — iris. has a peer who's been through it. Years later, when the member is stronger, iris. asks the quiet question that completes the arc: "Would you help someone just starting?"
Journey 2
Volunteers & community
01
Acquire — The ask that lands
iris. doesn't ask. iris. invites.
Most volunteer recruitment feels like begging. iris. knows who's ready — a member who's found their footing, a family member who's been through it, a retired professional, a student who wants real work — and opens the door at the right moment, with the right invitation.
02
Engage — A role that fits
The right work for this volunteer, right now.
No 20-page onboarding PDF. iris. matches each volunteer to real people and real work — peer mentor, sighted guide, fundraising voice, policy advocate. The role fits their geography, their skills, their energy. Training comes with a conversation, not a webinar.
03
Retain — Impact you can feel
Volunteer churn is a hidden cost. iris. solves it.
iris. stays connected. "You helped Margaret find her peer mentor. You welcomed David's family into the network." The impact is named, felt, real. iris. grows the role with the person — mentor becomes leader, leader becomes advocate. The people who give, stay.
The Loop
A member iris. helped at their most disoriented moment becomes a volunteer iris. matches to the next person stepping into that same moment.
The ecosystem regenerates. CNIB grows from within. Engagement becomes retention becomes the most powerful acquisition engine CNIB has ever had.

Three Ways In

iris. meets people
wherever they need it most.

The moment of need happens in different places. iris. is built to be there for all of them.

Direct
Individuals & Families
Someone newly diagnosed, a family member searching for help, a person whose vision has changed — anyone can begin a conversation with iris. directly. No referral. No waitlist. No intake form.
"I didn't know any of this existed. My doctor never mentioned it." — Margaret, 68, Sudbury
Medical Referral
Ophthalmologists, Clinics & Hospitals
The diagnosis room is the most powerful moment to make a CNIB connection. Retinal specialists, diabetes clinics, and optometrists can refer patients to iris. via QR code or direct link — often before the patient leaves the building.
A retinal clinic in Vancouver places a QR code in their waiting room. Three patients per week begin a conversation with iris. — before they leave the appointment.
Caregivers & Counselors
Social Workers, OTs & Family
Occupational therapists, social workers, low vision counselors, and family caregivers are often the first to recognize that support is needed. iris. gives them a trusted, specific referral — not a generic website, but a guided conversation tailored to their client.
An OT working with a patient post-stroke uses iris. as their go-to referral for vision-related support — confident it will meet their client where they are.

Every path leads to the same place: a person with sight loss, connected to the support they need.

The Experience

A guide who
actually knows you.

Not a directory. Not a chatbot. A guide that learns who each member is — through conversation, over time, across the real moments of their life.

01
iris. listens first.
iris. opens with the human question, not the intake form. What brought you here? What's changed? What's been the hardest part? iris. builds a picture of the whole person before suggesting anything.
02
iris. matches precisely.
Based on diagnosis, geography, life stage, and what matters most right now, iris. surfaces two or three programs that are actually right — not a list of everything CNIB offers.
03
iris. takes the next step.
iris. doesn't end with information — it ends with action. A referral booked. A connection made. A follow-up scheduled. The member leaves having gained something real.
04
iris. stays.
iris. checks in as life changes. From newly diagnosed to engaged community member. From member to peer mentor. From helped to helper.

Not a one-time interaction. A lifelong guide.

The Full Journey

From helped
to helper.

iris. doesn't just connect members to CNIB once. iris. walks the whole road with them — from the first frightening moment to the point where they're strong enough to guide someone else.

1
Newly Diagnosed

2
Engaged Member
iris. checks in as needs evolve. New programs surface as life changes. The relationship deepens over time.
3

iris. recognizes when a member is ready to give back — and connects them to someone earlier in their journey.
4
Community Leader
The most powerful advocates for iris. are the people it's already helped. The community grows from within.
The volunteer pathway is built into iris.
iris. identifies members who are ready to give back — based on their journey, their engagement, and the skills they've developed. Peer mentorship. Sighted guide volunteering. Fundraising. Community leadership. iris. matches volunteers to roles the same way it matches members to programs: personally, specifically, at the right moment.
