The Experience

A guide who
actually knows you.

Not a directory. Not a chatbot. A guide that gets to know each member the way a good mentor would — through conversation, over time, across the real moments of their life.

iris. listens first.
iris. opens with the human question, not the intake form. What brought you here? What's changed? What's been the hardest part? iris. builds a picture of the whole person before suggesting anything.
iris. matches precisely.
Based on diagnosis, geography, life stage, and what matters most right now, iris. surfaces two or three programs that are actually right — not a list of everything CNIB offers.
iris. takes the next step.
iris. doesn't end with information. iris. ends with action. A referral booked. A connection made. A follow-up scheduled. The member leaves having gained something real.
iris. stays.
iris. checks in as life changes. From newly diagnosed to engaged community member. From member to mentor. From helped to helper.

Not a one-time interaction. A lifelong guide.

The Pilot

How do we reach the other 99%?

By going to where they already are.

The pilot tests two parallel paths to reach Canadians with sight loss who have never engaged with CNIB — and proves the model before national scale.

CNIB Direct
iris. is deployed to existing CNIB members and warm leads. This tests the onboarding experience, conversation quality, and program connection rates with people already in the CNIB orbit — before reaching net-new members.
Channel Partner Referral
iris. is embedded in two to three ophthalmology and diabetes clinic waiting rooms via QR code. This tests the cold-start experience — reaching people at the exact moment of diagnosis, before they've had a chance to navigate alone.
Months 1–2
Build & Brief
Finalize iris. conversation flows. Train on CNIB program knowledge. Brief clinical partners. Set success metrics.
Months 3–5
Pilot Live
Deploy to CNIB direct channel and two clinic partners. Monitor conversations, program referrals, and member feedback in real time.
Month 6
Read & Scale
Analyse results. Identify highest-performing channels. Present findings to CNIB leadership. Build national rollout plan.
What success looks like

A member who would never have found CNIB finds it — and takes a next step. A clinician who didn't know CNIB existed becomes a referral partner. A volunteer who was once a member brings in five more. The number of Canadians engaged with CNIB starts moving in the right direction for the first time.

The pilot also tests iris. as a volunteer engagement tool — identifying members ready to give back and connecting them to meaningful roles within the CNIB community.
