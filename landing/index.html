Meet iris.

The iris is the part of the eye that decides how much light to let in. When sight changes, the spectrum narrows. iris. holds every color.

iris.

Your guide for life with sight loss.
So you can get back to living.

When sight loss changes everything, knowing what's available to you shouldn't be another thing to figure out alone.

iris. is CNIB's member engagement guide. iris. gets to know each member — their diagnosis, their location, their stage of life, what matters to them — and connects them to the programs, community, and support built for exactly where they are. Personally. Specifically. At no cost.

The System

One framework. Three audiences. Infinite scale.

The same three stages for everyone CNIB's mission depends on — clients, volunteers, and partners. That's what makes it systematic. That's what makes it scalable.

the loop
01

Clients

iris. meets Canadians living with sight loss wherever they enter — a diagnosis room, a 2am search, a worried family call — and walks with them for life.

Acquire
Any entry point — clinic, search, or family — meets one warm conversation: "I'm so glad you found me."
Engage
Listens first. Connects to the two or three programs that are actually right, not a directory dump. Books the next step.
Retain
Checks in as life changes. Years later, asks the quiet question: "Would you help someone just starting?"
02

Volunteers

iris. identifies who's ready to give back, invites them at the right moment in the right role, and grows the relationship so they stay.

Acquire
Invites people ready to give back — starting with retained clients — at the right moment, in the right role.
Engage
Matches peer mentor, sighted guide, fundraising, advocacy — to life, skills, energy. Onboarded by conversation, not a PDF.
Retain
Impact is felt, not reported: "You helped Margaret find her peer." Mentors become leaders. Churn drops.
03

Partners

iris. gives ophthalmologists, clinics, caregivers, and counsellors a warm referral surface — one scan, one conversation — so iris. is present the moment sight loss is first named.

Acquire
A QR in a waiting room, a link in a social worker's toolkit. One scan puts a new member into iris.
Engage
iris. gives each partner a live view of their referrals — outcomes, handoffs, and when the loop closed.
Retain
Repeated successful referrals turn partners into advocates. The professional network grows from within.
The Loop
A member iris. helped at their most disoriented moment becomes a volunteer iris. matches to the next person stepping into that same moment.
The ecosystem regenerates. CNIB grows from within. Engagement becomes retention becomes the most powerful acquisition engine CNIB has ever had.
