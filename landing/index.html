The Gap

The support exists.
Most people never find it.

Sight loss arrives fast and without a roadmap. A diagnosis. A doctor's appointment. A moment in the kitchen when something doesn't look right. And then — nothing. No one tells you what comes next.

CNIB has been building programs, services, and community for Canadians with sight loss for over a hundred years. And yet, of the more than 1.5 million Canadians living with sight loss, fewer than 13,000 are currently engaged with what CNIB offers.

When sight loss changes everything, knowing what's available to you shouldn't be another thing to figure out alone.

iris. is CNIB's member engagement guide. iris. gets to know each member — their diagnosis, their location, their stage of life, what matters to them — and connects them to the programs, community, and support built for exactly where they are. Personally. Specifically. At no cost.
