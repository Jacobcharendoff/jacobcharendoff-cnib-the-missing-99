# iris. launch film — v1

**Format:** 2-minute keynote launch film
**Tone:** upbeat, optimistic, futuristic, energizing — a new era for CNIB
**Structure:** six-act Apple-launch build — Tension → Turn → Reveal → Capability → Thesis → Close
**Narration target:** ~130 words across 2:00. Silence carries the rest.
**Music:** original score. Three builds — pulse in at 0:25, tempo lock and first drop at 0:55, peak at 1:40, resolve at 2:00. Think M1 launch film or Vision Pro reveal — warmth, not tech-demo cold. Optimism, not triumph.
**Typography:** iris brand serif, lowercase always, generous negative space, slow fades.
**Hero thread:** Margaret. One actress, two moments, twelve months apart in-story. Seen at 0:25 as a new client. Seen at 1:40 as a mentoring volunteer. The film is her round trip.

## Production notes

- **Casting.** One actress carries the film. She must read as 68, not a younger actress aged up. Sudbury specificity matters — shoot there if possible. Natural light, real kitchen, real clinic waiting room.
- **Pacing.** Narration is deliberately sparse — ~130 words across 2:00. That's 65 wpm. Half the density of a typical ad. The silence is the product.
- **Score brief.** Three-act build. Open ambient (0:00–0:25). Pulse in (0:25–0:50). First drop and tempo lock at 0:55 — this is the iris reveal, has to land like the M1 tempo hit. Build through capability (1:05–1:40). Peak at thesis (1:40–1:55). Resolve at logo (1:55–2:00). Warmth over tech-cold. Optimism over triumph.
- **Typography.** iris brand serif, always lowercase, generous negative space, slow crossfades. Numbers appear as type cards, not UI screenshots, in Act 1. UI only appears in Act 4.
- **No stock. No aerials. No drone shots.** Every frame is a named person in a named place. Every number on screen is a real metric from the iris system.
- **Voice direction.** Third-person narrator. Calm confidence. Not salesy. Not warm-and-fuzzy. Think the M1 narrator — measured, declarative, lets the visuals carry the emotion. The narrator is not selling iris. The narrator is introducing her.
- **Banned language (already clean, noting for reference).** No "seamless," "platform," "solution," "AI-powered," "revolutionary," "cutting-edge," "leverage," "journey," "inspirational."
