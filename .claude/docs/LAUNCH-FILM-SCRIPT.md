# iris. launch film — v2

**Format:** 2-minute launch film, 100% AI-produced
**Tone:** upbeat, optimistic, futuristic, energizing — a new era for CNIB
**Structure:** six-act Apple-launch build — Tension → Turn → Reveal → Capability → Thesis → Close
**Narration target:** ~130 words across 2:00. Silence carries the rest.
**Music:** original score from Suno. Three builds — pulse in at 0:25, tempo lock and first drop at 0:55, peak at 1:40, resolve at 2:00. Reference: M1 launch film or Vision Pro reveal. Warmth, not tech-cold. Optimism, not triumph.
**Typography:** iris brand serif, lowercase always, generous negative space, slow fades. Rendered programmatically via Remotion.
**Visual language:** 70% typography on black, 20% real iris UI (screen-recorded from iris-2-bay), 10% abstract AI stills (hands, light, QR, windows). **No human faces.** Margaret is a voice and a hand — never a face. This is deliberate and is the Apple move.
**Through-line:** One hand. Same ring. Same pink nail polish. Act 2 — her hand holds a clinic card and scans a QR code as a new client. Act 5 — the same hand types on a tablet, sending an iris match to someone else. She has become the volunteer. We see it through a detail on her finger, not her face.

## Production sequence

1. **VO generation.** I call `/api/tts` on iris-2-bay with the VO script. Output: `iris-launch-vo.mp3`, ~2 min. Committed to repo.
2. **Music generation.** You run the Suno prompt. Download the track. Drop the MP3 in Downloads. I pick it up.
3. **Midjourney stills.** You run the four MJ prompts with consistency locking. Download the four images. Drop them in Downloads. I pick them up.
4. **UI captures.** I drive Chrome MCP to iris-2-bay and record the six UI clips.
5. **Remotion build.** I write the React scenes for all typography beats in the sandbox, render to MP4 clips.
6. **Assembly.** FFmpeg in sandbox — concatenate all video clips to the 2:00 timeline, mix VO over music, output `iris-launch-v1.mp4` to Downloads.
